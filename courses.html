English

Great ideas can only be articulated and conveyed through language. Developing a strong command of the English Language is vital to the academic development of your child. Language is the foundation on which the building blocks of education rest upon.

Acknowledging this, Get Smart follows a rigorous teaching programme that comprehensively explains English Grammar and instils the art of creative writing.

Whatever the academic level of students, we will form a tailored learning plan that will develop their understanding and use of English grammar, as well as sharpening their writing skills.

Maths

Sound understanding of mathematics is without question crucial to academic success; it forms the basis of logic, reasoning and analysis.

Get Smart makes use of a neuro-scientific algorithm to give a solid indication of a student’s knowledge of all key mathematical concepts. Based on this score, a tailored learning plan is developed to explain and clarify all mathematical topics over the course of the student’s study programme.

Whether it is a child starting their studies and learning to count, add and subtract, or whether it is in preparation for a 11+ or GCSE exam, Get Smart has the solution for the equation.
