Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Home
  2. FAQ's
Which subjects do you offer tuition for?

Currently, we offer one-to-one tuition for Maths and English only.

Will my child be taught in accordance with the UK curriculum?

Yes. The entire learning programme has been compiled by experienced Teachers who are currently still teaching in UK schools.

How will my child’s academic abilities be assessed?

We employ two primary methods to assess ability:

(1) we utilise software that uses neuro-scientific algorithm to diagnose academic prowess, and

(2) a traditional question and answer assessment.

How will this take place online?

We conduct all lessons via Zoom or Skype. Preferably Zoom as it has additional features to aid learning such as whiteboard.

Will I have to pay to use Zoom or Skpe?

No, both facilities are free to use. Please download and install on your computer or laptop. Or contact our office who will advise.

My child has some learning difficulties, will this be an issue?

Our teachers are trained to deal with all types of children including those with learning difficulties. It is the job and passion of all our teachers to assist with academic learning and overcome these challenges.

My child seems to avoid studying and does not have a study routine, how will you ensure that he benefits from one-to-one tuition?

We would argue that one-to-one tuition is the precise solution in these circumstances, our tutors will be wholly focused on the child’s requirements and will communicate in a friendly and engaging way so that the child begins to enjoy his/her studies.

How will I know whether my child is making progress?

After the first round of sessions, his/her personal tutor will draft a short to long term study programme in which all aims and objectives will be set out. This will be relayed to you. The student will take assessments periodically to check his/her progress.

Will I be given reports or analysis of my child’s progression?

Yes, we use a software which utilises a neuro-scientific algorithm to give an up to score of the child’s progression. Full scores for each topic and sub-category will be recorded and will be presented to parents on a weekly or monthly basis. In addition to that, an accompanying written report.

My child is performing rather well in school, is there any need for him to take one-to-one tuition?

We would encourage all children to take up one-to-one tuition irrespective of their academic levels. Once assessed, a child’s progression will only be accelerated with one-to- one tuition. The aim is to help such students excel and keep moving forward to greater heights.

Can you guarantee that my child will pass his/her 11+ paper or gains great GCSE’s?

Our objective is to teach and facilitate learning, we are absolutely confident that children will benefit immeasurably from one-to-one tuition and that they will progress academically much sooner than if they were not receiving one-to-one tuition. Statistics show that a child receiving private tuition is more likely to pass their 11+ paper or gain better GCSE’s.

Will my child practise past exam papers?

We will regularly test students with past exam papers as part of ongoing reassessments.

What if I am not entirely comfortable with the assigned tutor?

We can offer another tutor. It is very important that both the student and the parent is at ease with their respective teacher.

How do I cancel a lesson and will there be a charge?

We will ask you to notify us in advance of any cancellation for whatever reason and we will rearrange the lesson for you. However, if this regularly occurs then we may not be able to reschedule the lesson and a charge may apply, we will inform you of this.

What if I can not connect with my tutor online?

We have an administrator overseeing all lessons. If there are any issues regarding this or anything else, you can contact the administrator.

Who is responsible that the tutor’s lesson starts on-time and that all runs smoothly?

The entire process is managed from our office. Our admin staff will deal with arranging and rearranging as well as all other administrative duties.

What safeguarding measures do you take?

All Tutors will be given STRICT INSTRUCTIONS to comply with all company guidelines and procedures to ensure the privacy and safety of all students. It is very important that you communicate all amendments and changes to schedules with our office as well as any matter that gives cause for concern, even if precautionary.

Do you have only female tutors?

Yes

Will your office deal with schedules and all communication?

We will communicate all scheduled lessons to you by email. Our office can also give you a call shortly before your next lesson upon request. All communication must take place with the office regarding schedule changes. In order to safeguard your child, it is imperative that everything related to lesson schedules is communicated with the office. No online session should take place without the knowledge of the office even with your regular tutor.

As a family can we schedule a class for more than one child at the same time with the same tutor that is to say a group session?

In these instances, we allow upto 4 students to participate in a single lesson at the same time even if the children are of different academic abilities and ages. Please enquire as to the charge

How will we pay for the lessons?

You will pay in advance for the planned lessons for the forthcoming month.

If there are any issues who can we contact?

At all times, you can contact the office by email or phone with regard to any issues, enquiries or complaints.
