Why Choose Get Smart

Focusing on the core subjects of Maths and English, we develop a tailored learning programme in accordance with the UK curriculum for each student. Upon enrollment an in-house diagnostic assessment conducted by your personal tutor will serve as an indicator of the student’s academic level and a thorough lessons plan will be created accordingly.

All lessons will be conducted via an online video sharing platform; this will allow students.

and tutors to communicate easily and share a live private-class screen to present teaching materials with an interactive whiteboard to carry out all exercises simulating a school classroom.

A systematic lesson and homework help plan will be followed by each student. All progress will be logged online and our smart software utilizing a sophisticated neuro-scientific algorithm will give an updated academic score accesible to parents at any time. In addtion to that, full reports will be sent to parents periodically informing them of their children’s progress.
